Finding the right information can be tough. Let us help.

USAGov helps you navigate a complex world of government information and services, equipping you with the tips and tools to make your life a bit easier. We give you access to resources from hundreds of federal agencies, departments, and programs — in English and Spanish. Whether you need to register to vote, apply for a passport, or contact a member of Congress, USAGov is here for you — on and offline.

What resources are available from USAGov?

Explore USAGov to find information and services related to housing, health, immigration, employment, benefits, and more.

Can I speak with someone by phone or chat?

Call 1-844-USA-GOV1 or go to USA.gov/chat to connect with specialists who can answer your questions.

Where can I find educational resources?

Go to Kids.USA.gov to get the latest activities, worksheets, and lesson plans for students, teachers, and parents.

Where can I access information in Spanish?

Visit GobiernoUSA.gov for resources on immigration, housing, financial assistance, and more — all in Spanish.
